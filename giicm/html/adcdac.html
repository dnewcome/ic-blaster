TL507, TL507C

7-bit PWM output analog-to-digital converter.
Only one of the two power supply pins should be used, 3.5V < VCC < 6V; 8V < VDD < 18V. At VCC=5V the analog input range is 1.3V < AIN < 3.9V. 
     +---+--+---+
  EN |1  +--+  8| /RST
 CLK |2  TL    7| VDD
 GND |3  507   6| VCC
/OUT |4        5| AIN
     +----------+

        +----------+
