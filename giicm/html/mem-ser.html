Memory: Serial RAM, EEPROM and clocks

DS1202

Real-time clock with 3-wire serial interface and 24 bytes RAM. 
    +---+--+---+
    |1  +--+  8| VCC
 X1 |2   DS   7| CLK
 X2 |3  1202  6| DQ
GND |4        5| /RST
    +----------+

2401

I2C 128x8 EEPROM with write protect.
Address is 1010xxx where x can be specified by the A0-2 inputs. 
    +---+--+---+
 A0 |1  +--+  8| VCC
 A1 |2        7| WP
 A2 |3  2401  6| SCL
GND |4        5| SDA
    +----------+

2404

I2C 2x256x8 EEPROM with write protect.
Address is 1010xxy where x can be specified by the A1-2 inputs, and y selects the 256-byte bank to use. A0 has no function, but must be connected to GND or VCC. 
    +---+--+---+
 A0 |1  +--+  8| VCC
 A1 |2        7| WP
 A2 |3  2404  6| SCL
GND |4        5| SDA
    +----------+
